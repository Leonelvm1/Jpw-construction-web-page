Who we are

At JPW, we've dedicated 23 years to transforming dreams into reality, creating spaces that reflect each client's imagination. Our experience working with leading brands like IKEA and Home Depot has allowed us to offer innovative, high-quality solutions. Each project is an opportunity to exceed expectations and transform your vision into a unique experience. Thank you for trusting us over two decades of work and continuous growth!

Our mission is simple: to exceed our clients' expectations by delivering unique solutions and exceptional service. In every cut, sand, and finish, we carry the spirit of Bethlehem: tradition, quality, and a passion for craftsmanship.
